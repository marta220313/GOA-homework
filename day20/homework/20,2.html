Sports are physical activities that involve skill, competition, and often teamwork.




They are played for enjoyment, fitness, and entertainment, and can range from individual endeavors like running or swimming to team sports such as soccer, basketball, and rugby. In addition to promoting physical health, sports help foster mental discipline, teamwork, and perseverance. They are an integral part of cultures worldwide, bringing people together and offering a sense of community and shared passion. Whether at the professional level or as a casual hobby, sports offer countless benefits that extend beyond just physical fitness, influencing social and emotional well-being as well.
